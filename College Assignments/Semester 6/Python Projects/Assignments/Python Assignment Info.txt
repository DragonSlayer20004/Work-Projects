Semester 6 at Otterbein university course COMP 1800 Computing in Python

Short Course Description:
A first course in computer programming for non-majors. Data types, control flow, objects, methods, classes, lists, and dictionaries
are some of the topics covered. Problems are taken from a wide range of domains such as numerical computation, cryptography, 
statistics, image processing, fractal geometry, data analytics, and simulation.

There are 9 assignments total. All assignments done predominantly in Python.
